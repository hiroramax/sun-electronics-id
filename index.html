About Us

Powering a Brighter Indonesia

PT Samahita Utama Nusantara (SUN Electronics) is a renewable energy system integrator that designs, installs, and maintains clean energy solutions across Indonesia — from urban centers to the remotest corners of the archipelago.

We integrate multiple technologies — solar panels, micro hydro, inverters, and battery storage — into a single system tailored to your needs. From consultation, system design, installation, to real-time monitoring, we handle it all.
